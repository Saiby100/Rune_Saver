Gain bonus movement speed for 10 seconds, ramping up to 45% over 1.5 seconds, while moving toward enemy champions (3000 range, includes enemy champions out of vision). Activating Predator will not break stealth.

After ramping up, your next attack or damaging ability against an enemy champion deals 40-120 (+0.2 of bonus attack damage) (+10% of ability power) bonus Adaptive damage and ends the effect (depending on level).

Deals either physical or magic damage depending on your bonus statistics, defaulting based on the origin of the effect.

Cooldown: 90-60 seconds (depending on level)
